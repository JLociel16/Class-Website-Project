Mission

THE POINT CDC is an extraordinary venue for your special event or meeting. With several unique spaces available for rental, a convenient location, catering service, staffing, THE POINT is an ideal choice for entertaining needs.
We host a variety of events, community meetings, live events, wedding receptions, rehearsals, dinners, corporate receptions, holiday parties and non-profit events. We can accommodate several hundred guests throughout the entire space depending on your needs. Our Events and Space Rental team can manage all the details of your event, including décor, entertainment and other planning needs.

Important Information:

  • A signed contract and date-hold deposit of 25% of your total is due upon signing must be received to reserve your date and time.
  • All rentals include 1 hour set up time and 1 hour breakdown time.
  • Renter shall not have access to space at any time other than during the start/end time hours on event date, unless Renter receives written permission from POINT staff
  • Must be 18 years or older to rent and state issued I.D. is required at signing.
